# From the tragedies

## H

- Thou art a fishmonger
- Thou tedious fool
- Thou whoreson mad fellow
- Thou art unfit for any place but hell
- Why, thou wretched fool
- Thou art a gross lard-loving pig
- Thou art a provincial rose (mocking)
- These tedious old fools

## K

- Thou whoreson dog, thou slave, thou cur
- Detested kite, thou liest
- Thou art a vile thing
- Thou art a boil, a plague sore
- Thou art an eater of broken meats
- Thou hast little wit in thy bald crown
- Thou art nothing
- A tailor made thee
- Thou art a knave, a rascal, an eater of broken meats
- A base, proud, shallow, beggarly knave
- A lily-livered, action-taking knave
- A whoreson, glass-gazing, superserviceable, finical rogue
- One-trunk-inheriting slave
- Son and heir of a mongrel bitch

## L

- Out of my sight, thou dost infect mine eyes
- Thou art a serving-creature
- Thou art a slave, a beggar
- Thou art as valiant as the worm

## M

- Thou cream-faced loon
- Go prick thy face and over-red thy fear
- Liar and slave
- The devil damn thee black, thou cream-faced loon
- What soldiers, patch?
- Thou art a traitor

## O

- You are pictures, mere show of duty
- Thou art a villain
- I am nothing if not critical
- Thou art a devil
- An old black ram is tupping your white ewe

## R

- Thou poisonous bunch-backed toad
- Thou lump of foul deformity
- Thou elvish-mark'd, abortive, rooting hog
- Thou that wast sealed in thy nativity the slave of nature
- Thou slander of thy mother's heavy womb
- Thou loathed issue of thy father's loins
- Thou rag of honour, thou dead element
- Out of my sight, thou dost infect mine eyes
- Thou art unfit for any place but hell
- A knot you are of damned bloodsuckers
- Foul devil, for God's sake hence

## T

- Away, thou issue of a mongrel bitch
- Thou cold-blooded slave
- Thou art a slave whom Fortune never clasped
- Thou art a castaway
- Thou sodden-witted lord
- Thou art a boil, a plague sore
- Thou crusty batch of nature
- Thou ruinous butt, thou whoreson indistinguishable cur
- Thou sodden-witted son of a whoreson

## C

- Thou art called a fat villain
- Thou art a traitor
- Thou art a villain
